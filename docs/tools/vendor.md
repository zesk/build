### `isPHPStorm` - Are we within the JetBrains PHPStorm application?

Are we within the JetBrains PHPStorm application?

#### Usage

    isPHPStorm

#### Exit codes

- `0` - within the PhpStorm terminal
- `1` - not within the PhpStorm terminal AFAIK
